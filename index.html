Indian Cuisine

Indian cuisine is a collection of various regional cuisines native to India. Each region has its own distinct culinary traditions and flavors.It is known for its diverse range of spices and cooking techniques.It includes a wide variety of dishes, from simple home-cooked meals to elaborate ceremonial foods such as biryani , dal , roti , and dosas.
